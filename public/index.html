Challenge

Residents don't know

Many people are unaware of the wide range of great services provided by their cities.

Can't imagine

Conceivably, a lot of people can’t even imagine that a city can offer such things — and for free?

How to discover

This is not a problem of search — it is a problem of discovery. How can anyone find something they don’t know exists?

Solution

Give people an application that recommends city services and events that they will love.

(Such as art, accessibility, sports, and festivals that personally apply to them.)

  • Every once in a while we ask you questions and tell you about something that is going on in the city that relates to you.

    (Example) Hey, are you into gardening?

  • When you attend events, we ask you a few questions for even feedback, or maybe just get to know you a little more.

    (YES) Cool! The City is giving away free compost this Saturday

  • We use data science to match your answers to event tags based on continuous confidence threshholds of your preferences.

    (NO) Would you like to get started? Some folks are giving free seedlings

  • We use an NLP pipeline to pull data and automatically tag events from publically posted calendars.

    (ATTENDED) Awesome! Would you like to tell the City what you thought
